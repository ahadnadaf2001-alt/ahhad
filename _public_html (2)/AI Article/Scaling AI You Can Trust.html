Event Overview

In a rapidly evolving digital landscape, CIOs are under increasing pressure to unlock AI's full potential, while navigating the complexities of secure data, regulatory compliance and scalable architectures. Understanding AI isn't just about technology; it's about reimagining how the enterprise operates, makes decisions and delivers value. To stay ahead, CIOs must move beyond buzzwords and develop a clear, actionable view of AI's capabilities, risks and long-term impact on the business.

At this exclusive roundtable, hosted by Intelligent CIO Middle East and powered by Kyndryl, senior technology leaders will come together to explore:

  • Agentic AI frameworks: How emerging models are reshaping autonomy, decision-making and enterprise efficiency especially in high-stakes or regulated environments.
  • Data-secure resilience: Strategies to strengthen infrastructure, ensure privacy and future-proof the data ecosystem.
  • Regulatory readiness: What upcoming AI regulations mean for CIOs and how to stay ahead without slowing innovation.

As a global leader in IT infrastructure and transformation services, Kyndryl can credibly speak to secure deployment, data management and operational resilience for enterprise AI. By integrating AI across infrastructure, applications, and workflows, Kyndryl empowers enterprises to move from reactive to predictive operations, delivering greater agility, efficiency, and innovation.

What to Expect

  • Start the day with a networking breakfast before the discussion begins
  • Insights into the evolving role of AI in IT modernisation
  • Practical approach to AI integration and governance
  • Peer-to-peer discussion with CIOs, CTOs and Digital Transformation leaders

The question is no longer if to adopt AI, but how to scale it responsibly.

This closed-door session will provide a collaborative forum to exchange insights with peers, benchmark your AI journey and shape the standards for enterprise AI adoption.
